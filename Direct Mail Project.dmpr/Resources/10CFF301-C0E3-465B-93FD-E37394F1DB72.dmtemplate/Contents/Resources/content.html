Getting Started

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

You can select a template for your email by clicking the Choose Template button above.

Once you’ve chosen a template, you’ll edit your message right here. Use the toolbar to insert content (like images, text, and buttons), add links, or personalize your message. Click the Send button in the top-right corner of this window when your email is ready to go. Your work is automatically saved as you go along.

If you have any questions, click Help in the menu bar for documentation and live chat support.
